Board Game Lending

Each of our four branches has a different selection of board games for you to borrow. View an informational brochure.

Borrowing Guidelines
  • Games can be borrowed for 21 days.
  • Games are not eligible for renewal and may not be placed on Hold.
  • Games must be returned to the lending library.
  • You may borrow 2-3 games at a time.
